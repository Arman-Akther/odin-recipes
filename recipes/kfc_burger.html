The Classic KFC Burger

Algorithm for the Zinger Burger!

1) Slice up chicken breasts, then marinate with pepper, salt, mustard and/or spice powder and Worcestershire sauce. Leave the chicken over night (4 hours minimum).

2) Prepare batter by beating an egg and 2 tablespoons of water.

3) Place marinated chicken in flour and coat. Dip into egg and then into breadcrumbs until well coated.

4) On medium to high heat deep fry chicken in hot oil, wait till golden brown and crispy.

5) Stack burger with lettuce and mayonnaise inside lightly toasted buns. Voila!

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breasts
  • sauce: pepper, salt, mustard/spice powder, Worestershire sauce
  • 1 egg
  • flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • oil
  • lettuce
  • mayonnaise
  • toasted buns
